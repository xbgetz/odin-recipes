Grilled Cheese

Description:

Grilled cheese is a griddled cheese sandwich.

It is cooked in a pan with butter on both sides of the bread until it is crispy and the cheese is melty..

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Bread
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat pan
  2. Put cheese between bread
  3. Add butter to pan or directly to bread
  4. Grill on medium low heat until both sides are golden brown and crispy and the cheese is melted
