Lasagna

Description:

Scrambled eggs are a smooth creamy egg dish.

They are cooked in butter over low heat in a pan until silky smooth.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat pan on low heat
  2. Mix eggs with a fork in a bowl while pan is preheating
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Slowly cook and work the eggs with a spatula making sure they do not sit cooking in one spot for too long
